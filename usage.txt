Usage:  ass-restyler [clause]... <input.ass >output.ass
        ass-restyler --help

Clauses:
    Style:Property=Value
    Style:Property+=Value  (if it makes sense for the property)
    Style:Property-=Value  (if it makes sense for the property)

Example:
    ass-restyler Default:MarginV=335 Default:Fontsize+=10 <input.ass >output.ass
